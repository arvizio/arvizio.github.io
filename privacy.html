Arvizio Immerse 3D Privacy Policy

Purpose

The Arvizio Immerse 3D Privacy Policy describes the privacy practices of Arvizio’s software platform and applications. Arvizio Inc. Is a Canadian corporation based in Ottawa, governed by the laws of Canada and the laws of Ontario.

 

Scope

This policy applies to the Arvizio Immerse 3D software platform including Immerse 3D applications, Arvizio Director application and Arvizio Cloud Services.

 

The Arvizio Immerse 3D solution provides 3D collaboration software to enterprise customers including video, audio and mixed and augmented reality sharing capabilities.

 

Arvizio’s software does not collect or store any personal information as our customers deploy and run Arvizio Director and Immerse 3D application software using their own on-prem infrastructure. The Arvizio Cloud Service does not store customer data and only relays session data between devices over encrypted links.

The following description provides information regarding how Immerse 3D software utilizes audio and video capabilities of XR devices.

 

·         No recordings of audio are made by Arvizio and the XR device microphone is used only for real-time group collaboration sessions within the application. Arvizio Inc.  and its employees and contractors do not have the ability to listen to the user’s audio unless they join a network session as a visible and named participant. If a user does not wish to have their audio transmitted in a network session, the user can enable the in-app mute feature or elect not to join a network session.

·         No recording of video by Arvizio is made on the XR device cameras; the user explicitly records for their own use. Remote video capabilities are used only for real-time group collaboration sessions both locally and across multiple locations. Arvizio’s employees and contractors do not have the ability to connect to the user’s video feed unless they are invited to join the session as a visible and named participant.  If needed, the user can block the video feed transmission and or disconnect from the session at any point of time.
